GoRoam helps you find better places faster—discover nearby gems, share quick photo reviews, and save your favorites, with simple privacy controls when you want them.

Our mission

Most review apps are noisy, ad-heavy, non-interactive and outdated. GoRoam is place-first: a lightweight way to capture where you went, what you loved, and what you’d tell a friend—without the clutter. We focus on signal over noise, so you can decide where to go next in seconds.

What you can do

Discover & Save

  • Browse by category (Coffee Restaurants Adventure Entertainment Fitness).
  • See posts from people you follow and spots nearby.
  • Save places you love to revisit later.

Share Quickly

  • Post a photo, a sentence, and a place tag—done.
  • Mention places to keep your map tidy.
  • Comment and reply with clean, threaded conversations.

Keep Control

  • Private Account mode shows posts only to approved followers.
  • Edit or delete your posts and comments anytime.
  • Like/unlike with clear counts—no mystery.

Built for Real Life

  • Fast, simple UI focused on places—no clutter.
  • Optional location filters to surface relevant spots.
  • Thoughtful notifications so you don’t miss what matters.

How it works

1) Discover

Open GoRoam to see nearby picks and categories. Tap a place to view quick takes, photos, and tips from people you trust.

2) Share

Snap a photo (optional), write one or two lines, tag the spot. That’s it—your post helps friends decide fast.

3) Revisit

Save places you love. Your lightweight lists become a personal map of go-to coffee, bites, trails, and more.

Privacy at a glance

  • Your audience, your choice: Private Account limits content to approved followers.
  • Control your footprint: Delete posts/comments in-app; account deletion is available in Settings.
  • Location is optional: You can disable device location in OS settings; some features may be limited.
  • Read the full Privacy Policy for details on data, retention, and your rights.

Privacy questions? Email support@approamly.com.

Safety & moderation

FAQ

Is GoRoam free?

Yes. Some optional features may evolve over time, but discovering and sharing places is free.

Do I need location enabled?

No. You can use GoRoam without device location, but nearby and map features work best with it on.

How do I delete my account?

Use the in-app option in Settings → Delete Account, or email support@approamly.com.

What services power GoRoam?

We use trusted providers like Firebase and Google Maps/Places. See Privacy for details.
