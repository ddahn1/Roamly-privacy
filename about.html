About GoRoam

Last updated: September 2025

GoRoam helps you discover places, share quick reviews, and keep track of your favorites—privately and effortlessly. Whether it’s a cozy coffee shop, a hidden hiking trail, or a late-night food spot, GoRoam makes it easy to capture your experience and find where to go next.

What you can do

Discover & Save

  • Browse by category (Coffee, Restaurants, Adventure, Entertainment, Fitness).
  • See posts from people you follow and spots nearby.
  • Save places you love to revisit later.

Share Quickly

  • Post a photo, a sentence, and a tag—done.
  • Mention places to keep your map tidy.
  • Comment and reply with clean, threaded conversations.

Keep Control

  • Private account mode shows posts only to approved followers.
  • Edit or delete your posts and comments anytime.
  • Like/unlike with clear counts—no mystery.

Built for Real Life

  • Fast, simple UI focused on places—no clutter.
  • Optional location filters to surface relevant spots.
  • Thoughtful notifications so you don’t miss what matters.

Privacy at a glance

  • You control who can see your content via Private Account settings.
  • Delete posts/comments in-app; account deletion is available in Settings.
  • Place and region tags are optional—use them when useful.
  • Read the full Privacy Policy for details on data, retention, and your rights.
